# Third-Party Assets

Free asset packs and tools for prototyping and development.

## 2D Sprite Sheets (Top-Down)

### [Ninja Adventure Asset Pack](https://pixel-boy.itch.io/ninja-adventure-asset-pack) — pixel-boy

50+ characters with animations, 30+ monsters with animations, 9 bosses, 60+ items, tilesets, 100+ sound effects, 37 music tracks. 16x16 pixel size. The most complete single pack — enough to prototype a full game without any other assets.

- **License:** CC0 (no attribution required)
- **Format:** PNG sprite sheets, WAV/OGG audio
- **Size:** 82 MB

### [Universal LPC Spritesheet Character Generator](https://liberatedpixelcup.github.io/Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator/)

Web-based tool that generates custom characters from the Liberated Pixel Cup asset library. Mix and match body types (male, female, muscular, teen, child), hair, armor, weapons, and equipment. Walk, attack, spellcast, thrust, shoot, and hurt animations. Best option for generating a wide variety of distinct characters from one source.

- **License:** CC-BY / GPL (attribution required)
- **Pixel size:** 32x32
- **Source:** [LPC Collection on OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/content/liberated-pixel-cup-0)

### [Anokolisa's Pixel Crawler Pack](https://anokolisa.itch.io/free-pixel-art-asset-pack-topdown-tileset-rpg-16x16-sprites)

500+ sprites, 3 heroes, 8 enemies, 50 weapons. Dungeon crawler / roguelike style. Actively updated. Extended version ($10) adds more content.

- **License:** Free (custom license, check page)
- **Pixel size:** 16x16

### [Zelda-Like Tilesets and Sprites](https://opengameart.org/content/zelda-like-tilesets-and-sprites) — ArMM1998

Overworld, cave, and interior tilesets plus a swordsman character with walk/attack animations and a character template. The most directly Zelda-feeling art style.

- **License:** CC0 (no attribution required)
- **Pixel size:** 16x16
